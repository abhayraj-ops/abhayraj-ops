### 🌐 About OpenEMIS

> *"OpenEMIS collects and reports data on schools, students, teachers and staff — a royalty-free system customizable to the needs of UNESCO member countries."* — [UNESCO SDG4 Knowledge Hub](https://www.unesco.org/sdg4education2030/en/knowledge-hub/openemis)

I contribute to the [OpenEMIS Core](https://github.com/OpenEMIS/core) platform — a CakePHP-based EMIS that powers education data management across multiple countries worldwide.
